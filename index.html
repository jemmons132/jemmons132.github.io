Insurance · Hybrid Workplace Transformation
Most offices are designed for assumptions. This one was redesigned around data.

A major insurance organization needed to transform their headquarters to support hybrid work, improve employee satisfaction, and reduce their real estate footprint, without sacrificing function or experience. Sensor data made invisible patterns visible and guided every design decision.

63%Footprint reduction
7New flex rooms created
    What the data revealed
  • Most spaces used at under 25% capacity during business hours — validating the footprint reduction without employee impact
  • Regardless of room size, most meetings had only 2–3 attendees — driving a right-sizing strategy that converted underused areas into seven flex rooms
  • A beautifully designed community space used less than 5% of the time — sensor data surfaced what surveys couldn't and redirected investment where it was actually needed
